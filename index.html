Summary

I am a web developer with experience working on both front end and back end projects, including extensive exposure to MEAN stack JavaScript technologies and modern, responsive CSS design.

My main aim in my work is to work with others, be they other developers, non technical team members or clients, to determine the most appropriate solution to a given set of user needs and using it to create a product that is intuitive and powerful. My recent personal development projects include a small app to allow a player of the game Android Netrunner (well, me, but it could be used by another player) to track their wins and losses with certain decks against others.
